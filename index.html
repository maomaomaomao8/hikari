Hikari 光

When my son Juli was a newborn, I spent countless nights awake feeding him alone in the dark. The silence felt heavy — like the rest of the world had disappeared and I was the only one still awake.

Then I'd watch the sunrise and feel something shift. The night was ending. I had made it through.

Hikari came from that feeling. I wanted to build something that shows you — in that exact moment — that you are not alone. Somewhere in the world, another parent is awake with their baby too. In Tokyo it might be evening. In London it might be midnight. But somewhere, it is always now.
